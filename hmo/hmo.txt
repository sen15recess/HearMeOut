Ever fell asleep with your earphones plugged in and then woke up with a phone call or an alarm blaring into your ears ? Or even worse, managed to remove your earphones in your sleep but forgot to unplug them from your phone diverting all your phone calls or alarms to your earphones making you miss all your appointments ?
Hear Me Out may just be the perfect solution to all your problems. Now, you can set a timer before you plug in your earphones and Hear Me Out will take care of the rest. Now go, have a carefree nap!

Note : When done using application, click on the RESET button to restore earphone functionality.
